Mon illustration pour les vainqueurs de la 'Parry Cup', tournoi dédié uniquement à Street Fighter III Third Strike, organisé par la SWGA au Neo Arcadia Game Center de Toulouse les 23 et 24 novembre 2013. The arcade game prize illustration i made for the winners of the 'Parry Cup', a tournament dedicated to Street Fighter III Third Strike organized by the SWGA crew at the Neo Arcadia Game Center in Toulouse on the 23rd and 24th of November 2013.
